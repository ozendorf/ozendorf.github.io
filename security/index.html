Account Deletion Request

This page is provided by Nawa (developed by Fluent Flow) to help you request the deletion of your account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your Nawa account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Nawa mobile application on your device
  2. Navigate to the Login/Profile page
  3. If you are logged in, you will see account management options
  4. Select the option to delete your account
  5. Confirm your request to delete your account
  6. You will receive a confirmation message once the deletion process is initiated

Alternative Method: If you are unable to access the app, you can contact our support team directly through the app's support channels or by emailing us at the contact information provided below.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

  • User account information (email address, authentication credentials)
  • User profile data
  • Learning progress and activity history
  • Chat conversation history
  • Audio recordings and transcriptions
  • Device registration information
  • App preferences and settings
  • In-app purchase history (associated with your account)

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and compliance purposes, certain data may be retained for a limited period:

  • Transaction Records: Financial transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by applicable financial regulations
  • Legal Compliance: Data may be retained if required by law, court order, or to comply with legal obligations
  • Security Logs: Anonymized security and access logs may be retained for up to 90 days for security purposes

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted:

  • You will lose access to all your learning progress
  • You will not be able to recover any saved data
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Nawa again in the future

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Contact Information

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance with the process, please contact our support team:

Support: Available through the in-app support feature

Response Time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 5 business days
