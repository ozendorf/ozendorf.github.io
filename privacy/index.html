Nawa Application: Learn Arabic with AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15/02/2026

Introduction

Nawa ("we," "us," or "our") is a language learning application that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to help you learn Arabic. Our app connects to third-party AI services to provide personalised language lessons, translations, conversation practice, and feedback. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we collect it, how we use it, and with whom we share it — including our use of third-party AI services.

By using Nawa, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Before any of your data is sent to a third-party AI service, the app will ask for your explicit consent.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

1.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Text input and prompts: Words, phrases, sentences, and questions you type or speak into the app for translation, lesson generation, or conversation practice.
  • Learning preferences: Language proficiency level, lesson topics, and personalisation settings you configure in the app.

1.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage data such as app features used, time spent in the app, and interaction patterns.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app configuration.
  • General location: Country or region-level location data (not precise GPS coordinates).
  • Performance metrics: Crash logs, error reports, and app performance data.
  • Log data: In the event of an error, we may collect log data including your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, app configuration, and timestamps.

2. How We Collect Data

  • Direct input: When you type or speak text into the app's learning features (e.g., translation requests, conversation practice, lesson prompts).
  • Automatic collection: Through analytics SDKs and system-level APIs that gather anonymous usage and device data while you use the app.
  • Error reporting: Through third-party crash-reporting tools when the app encounters an error.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Data Used
Providing AI-powered language learning features (translations, lessons, conversation practice, feedback) Text input, learning preferences
Improving app functionality and user experience Analytics data, performance metrics
Diagnosing and fixing errors Log data, crash reports, device information
Personalising your learning experience Learning preferences, usage patterns

4. Data Shared with Third-Party AI Services

Our app uses OpenAI's API services to power its AI-based language learning features. This section explains exactly what data is shared, who it is shared with, and how it is protected.

4.1 What Data Is Sent to OpenAI

When you use the app's AI-powered features (such as translation, conversation practice, or lesson generation), the following data may be sent to OpenAI:

  • Text input: The words, phrases, sentences, or questions you enter into the app.
  • Learning context: Your selected proficiency level and lesson topic, so the AI can tailor responses to your level.
  • Conversation history within a session: Previous messages in an active conversation thread, to maintain context during a learning session.

We do not send your name, email address, precise location, contacts, photos, or any other personal identifiers to OpenAI.

4.2 Who the Data Is Sent To

Your data is sent to:

  • OpenAI, L.L.C. — headquartered in San Francisco, California, USA. OpenAI provides the large language model (LLM) API that powers the app's AI features. You can review OpenAI's privacy practices at https://openai.com/privacy and their API data usage policy at https://openai.com/policies/api-data-usage-policies.

4.3 User Consent

Before any of your data is transmitted to OpenAI, the app will present a clear consent prompt explaining what data will be shared and with whom. You must grant explicit permission before the app sends any data to OpenAI. If you do not consent, the AI-powered features of the app will not be available, but you may still access any offline or non-AI features.

You may withdraw your consent at any time through the app's settings, which will stop any further data from being sent to OpenAI.

4.4 Third-Party Data Protection

OpenAI, as our third-party AI service provider, is contractually required to handle your data in accordance with their API data usage policies. Under OpenAI's API data usage policy:

  • Data sent through the API is not used to train or improve OpenAI's models.
  • Data is retained for a limited period (up to 30 days) for abuse and misuse monitoring, after which it is deleted.
  • OpenAI implements industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit and at rest.

We have taken reasonable steps to ensure that OpenAI provides the same or equal level of protection for your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

5. Analytics Data

We use analytics tools to collect anonymous information about app usage patterns. This may include:

  • App features you use and how you interact with them
  • Time spent in the app
  • Device type and operating system version
  • General location data (country/region level only)
  • App performance metrics

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users. We use this data solely to improve our app's functionality and user experience.

6. Log Data

Whenever you use our app, in the event of an error, we may collect data and information (through third-party products) on your device called Log Data. This Log Data may include your device's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, app configuration, the time and date of your use of the app, and other statistics.

7. Cookies

This app does not use cookies explicitly. However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use cookies to collect information and improve their services.

8. Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals for the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our Service;
  • To provide the Service on our behalf;
  • To perform Service-related services; or
  • To assist us in analysing how our Service is used.

These third parties may have access to limited data only to perform their tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. Our primary third-party service provider for AI functionality is OpenAI, as described in Section 4 above.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Privacy Policy:

  • Text input sent to OpenAI: We do not store your text input on our servers after it has been processed. OpenAI may retain API data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, after which it is deleted.
  • Analytics data: Retained in anonymised form for up to 24 months.
  • Log and crash data: Retained for up to 12 months for debugging purposes.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Consent withdrawal: You may withdraw your consent for data sharing with OpenAI at any time through the app's settings.
  • Data deletion: You may request deletion of any data we hold about you by contacting us at the email address below.
  • Access: You may request information about what data we have collected about you.

11. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

12. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All data transmitted to third-party services, including OpenAI, is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. If changes are significant (such as new data sharing with additional third parties), we will notify you within the app. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
